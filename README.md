---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Tools
* 📈 **Power BI:** Data Visualization & Interactive Reporting.
* 🧹 **Power Query:** Advanced ETL processes and data restructuring.
* 🔢 **DAX:** Complex measures and Time Intelligence.
* 🤖 **MCP Server:** Model Context Protocol for AI-Agent assisted modeling and automation.

---

## 🚀 Project Workflow

### 1️⃣ Data Modeling (Star Schema)
* 🏗️ Established a robust **Star Schema** with a central **Fact Table** (`All_Sales`) linked to optimized **Dimension Tables**.
* 📅 Developed a custom **Calendar Date Table** to support accurate Time-Intelligence functions and ensure zero data gaps.

### 2️⃣ Data Cleaning & Transformation (ETL)
* 🔍 Standardized fragmented datasets into a **Single Source of Truth**.
* 💰 **Budget Consolidation:** Successfully merged three distinct budget tables (2019, 2020, and 2021) into a single unified table, streamlining the calculation of Total Budget vs. Actuals.

### 3️⃣ AI-Assisted Analytics (MCP Integration)
* 💡 **Innovative Approach:** Integrated an **MCP Server** to bridge Power BI with an **AI Agent**.
* ⚡ **Efficiency:** Utilized the AI Agent to validate complex table relationships and automate the generation of DAX measures, reducing manual development time and minimizing syntax errors.

### 4️⃣ Business Intelligence & Key KPIs
Engineered dynamic measures to track core business health:
* 💵 **Core Metrics:** Total Sales, Total Profit, and Total Cost.
* 🔄 **Year-over-Year (YoY) Analysis:** Automated comparisons between current and previous year's performance to detect growth trends.
* 📈 **Profitability Deep-Dive:** Analysis of margins across different product lines and timeframes.

### 5️⃣ Predictive Analysis (What-If Scenarios)
* 🔮 Implemented **What-If Parameters** to empower stakeholders with simulation capabilities.
* 🎯 **Dynamic Forecasting:** Visualize revenue impact if sales were to increase by **5%** or more, aiding in proactive strategic planning.
